Peter Saville

Graphic designer

Peter Saville is a renowned British graphic designer, art director, and co-founder of the legendary independent UK label Factory Records

Peter Saville’s unique approach to design has resulted in a significant contribution to culture. His work lies between art and design, making him a trailblazer in the industry. His influence can be seen in the work of many designers who followed in his footsteps.
